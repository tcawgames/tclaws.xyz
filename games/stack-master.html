About Stack Master

Stack Master is a fast-paced, addictive game where players must stack blocks as high as possible by perfectly timing their taps. The goal is to create a stable tower while the blocks move back and forth across the screen. With simple yet challenging gameplay and stunning visuals, players can test their precision and compete for high scores.

About Me

Who I Am

Hello! I'm TClaws, a passionate 3D artist and printing enthusiast. I've been immersed in the world of 3D modeling and printing for over 5 years, constantly exploring new techniques and pushing the boundaries of what's possible with this amazing technology.

My journey started with a simple desktop printer and has evolved into a full-fledged passion for creating intricate models, functional prints, and artistic pieces that blend form and function.

My Mission

I believe that 3D printing is more than just a hobby - it's a gateway to innovation and creativity. My mission is to share my knowledge, inspire others to explore this field, and create unique pieces that showcase the potential of additive manufacturing.

Through my work, I aim to demonstrate how 3D printing can solve real-world problems, create beautiful art, and bring imaginative concepts to life.

My 3D Printing Process

1

Concept & Design

Every project begins with an idea. I sketch concepts and plan the design before moving to digital modeling. This phase involves considering functionality, aesthetics, and printability.

2

3D Modeling

Using software like Blender, Fusion 360, and ZBrush, I create detailed 3D models. This is where the magic happens - turning concepts into digital reality with precision and creativity.

3

Slicing & Preparation

Before printing, models are prepared using slicing software. I optimize settings for layer height, infill, supports, and other parameters to ensure the best possible print quality.

4

Printing & Post-Processing

The actual printing process can take hours or even days. After printing, I carefully remove supports, sand surfaces, and sometimes apply paints or finishes to achieve the desired look.
